Purpose of This Page

This website was created as a tribute to Edwin Sifuna, recognizing his leadership, courage, and contribution to Kenya’s political landscape.

The goal is to highlight his impact, share inspiration, and demonstrate appreciation for leaders who stand for truth, accountability, and service to the people.

About the Creator

This tribute page was designed and developed as a personal project to showcase both admiration for leadership and web development skills using HTML and CSS.

It reflects creativity, respect for impactful leadership, and a passion for technology.
